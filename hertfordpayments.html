Detailed BIN Lookup

BIN Lookup provides issuer identification, card type detection (debit/credit/prepaid), and country of origin. It helps in applying dynamic fees, routing decisions, and preventing unauthorized transactions by cross-referencing with watchlists.

Detailed Token Vault

Our Token Vault uses encryption standards like AES-256, supports tokenization across channels, and enables secure data sharing with partners. It reduces PCI scope for merchants and facilitates seamless updates for expired cards.

Detailed 3D Secure

Implements 3DS2 protocol with frictionless authentication, risk-based analysis, and biometric support. Reduces chargebacks by up to 70%, complies with PSD2 regulations, and integrates with major card schemes like Visa and Mastercard.
